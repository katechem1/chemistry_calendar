Caffeine C8H10N2O2

Caffeine is a natural chemical with stimulant effects. It's found in coffee, tea, and chocolate, works by blocking a chemical in the brain called adenosine, which makes us feel tired. When adenosine is blocked, other chemicals like dopamine and norepinephrine are released, making us feel more awake, alert, and focused.
This is why we shouldn't drink coffee or tea late at night, otherwise we won't be
able to sleep.
