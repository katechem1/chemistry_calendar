Fructose

Fructose, also known as fruit sugar, is one of the three monosaccharides. It is found in plants. We know it mainly from fruit, but also from honey. Compared to glucose, it is a weaker sugar and therefore healthier. One of the many Christmas traditions is apple slicing. Whether you eat it afterwards is up to you, but at least you will know that the sweet taste is also caused by fructose.
