Thymol C10H14O

Thymol is an aromatic substance that occurs naturally in plants such as thyme and oregano. Thymol has been used to embalm bodies due to its preservative properties. The thymol contained in thyme or oregano is widely used in the preparation of Christmas dishes such as roasted meats, stuffings or vegetable mixtures, contributing to the typical aromas and flavours of
festive food.
