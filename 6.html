Sucrose C12H22O11

Sucrose is a common sugar that occurs naturally in plants, especially sugar cane and sugar beet. Sucrose is one of the main sources of quick energy for the human body because it is quickly broken down into simple sugars in the body. Sucrose is the basis of most Christmas sweets such as gingerbread, marzipan, Christmas candy or traditional sweet drinks. Without it, Christmas baking would not be possible!
